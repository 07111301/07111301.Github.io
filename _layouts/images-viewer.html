How to Zoom in:

1. Double click on the image to zoom in to that point.
2. Or hold down the command key (mac) or the ctrl key (Windows) and click on the image.
3. Or hold down the plus key to increment the zoom.
4. Or hold down the command key (mac) or the ctrl key (Windows) and use the mouse wheel.

How to Zoom out:

1. Double click on the zoomed image to zoom out.
2. Or hold down the command + alt key (mac) or the ctrl + alt key (Windows) and click on the image.
3. Or hold down minus key to decrement the zoom.
4. Or hold down the command key (mac) or the ctrl key (Windows) and use the mouse wheel.
